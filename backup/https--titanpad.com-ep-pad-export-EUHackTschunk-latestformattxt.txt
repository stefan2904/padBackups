Bus

 * Fee: 4 EUR; The estimated charge refers to tickets purchased at the ticket machine. Tickets purchased on the bus will be sold at an extra surcharge. Make sure to have coins handy.
 * Hours: 5:00 AM - 11:25 PM; Bus leaves every 20 minutes towards Luxembourg.
 * Travel Time: 30 minutes
   
    The bus terminal is located at Level 0 the airport. You will need to take bus number 12 or 21 to get to the hotel. Get off at the Schuman bus stop and the hotel is a two minute walk from there. Take Rue Froissart at the roundabout and then Rue Juste Lipse (the first turn on the right) and the hotel is located at the bottom of the street.

Regional Train

 * Fee: 8.50 EUR
 * Hours: The train leaves every 20 minutes.
 * Travel Time: 40 minutes

    Take the train for Brussels Central / Brussel-Zuid / Bruxelles-Midi / Mons/Quevy and exit at the Central Station in Brussels. From there, take Metro 1 for Stockel, or Metro 5 for Herrmann-Debroux. Exit at Schuman Station. The hotel is a two minute walk from Schuman Station. Take Rue Froissart at the roundabout and then Rue Juste Lipse (the first turn on the right). The hotel is located at the bottom of the street.
